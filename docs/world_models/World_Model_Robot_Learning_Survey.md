# 📚 World Model for Robot Learning: A Survey
**Comprehensive Review of Architectures and Challenges**

**Links**: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00080)

---

## 🎯 Core Objective
- **Taxonomy**: Categorizing different world model architectures (RNNs, Transformers, Diffusion).
- **State-of-the-Art Review**: Analyzing the current capabilities and limitations of world models in robotics.
- **Identifying Gaps**: Highlighting the challenges of long-horizon prediction and action-conditioning.

---

## ⚙️ Key Technical Concepts
- **Architectural Evolution**: From early latent-space RNNs to modern high-dimensional Diffusion Transformers.
- **Action-Conditioning**: How to effectively integrate robot actions into the predictive model.
- **Evaluation Metrics**: Standardizing how we measure "accuracy" in a generative world model.

---

## 🤖 Robotics Relevance
- **Roadmap for Research**: Providing a guide for where the field is heading.
- **Comparison**: Allowing researchers to choose the right architecture for their specific robotic task.
- **Standardization**: Pushing for consistent benchmarks across different world model implementations.
