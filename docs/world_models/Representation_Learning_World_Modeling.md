# 🧠 Representation Learning to World Modeling
**The Evolution of AI Perception and Prediction**

**Links**: [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GBd7iuJkW08)

---

## 🎯 Core Objective
- **Conceptual Evolution**: Understanding the shift from static "snapshots" (representations) to dynamic "movies" (world models).
- **Predictive Power**: Exploring how the ability to predict the future is the key to true intelligence.
- **Bridging the Gap**: Linking VAEs and GANs to the modern world model paradigm.

---

## ⚙️ Key Technical Concepts
- **Static Representations**: Learning a compressed latent space of a single image (e.g., VAE).
- **Dynamic Prediction**: Predicting the next latent state given the current state and action.
- **The "World Model" Loop**: Perception $\rightarrow$ Representation $\rightarrow$ Prediction $\rightarrow$ Action.

---

## 🤖 Robotics Relevance
- **Intuition**: Giving robots an "intuitive" sense of how the world changes.
- **Planning**: Allowing robots to imagine multiple future scenarios to pick the best one.
- **Foundation**: Understanding the building blocks of all modern embodied AI agents.
