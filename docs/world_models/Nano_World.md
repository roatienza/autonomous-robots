# Nano World (arXiv:2605.23993)

> **Link**: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.23993)

## Overview
This paper introduces the "Nano World" framework, focusing on a minimalist implementation of future video prediction. It emphasizes the use of **diffusion forcing** to maintain temporal consistency in long-horizon video rollouts.

## Core Concept
- **Diffusion Forcing**: A method to prevent the "drift" typically seen in autoregressive video generation by combining diffusion models with forcing mechanisms.
- **Minimalist Design**: Prioritizes clarity and reproducibility over industrial-scale complexity.
