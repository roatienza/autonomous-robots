# 🧬 What are World Models? (Nature)
**Conceptual Framework for Cognitive Science and AI**

**Links**: [Nature](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-026-00820-5)

---

## 🎯 Core Objective
- **Conceptual Clarity**: Explaining the "World Model" concept to a broad scientific audience.
- **Cognitive Mapping**: Linking AI world models to how biological brains simulate the environment.
- **Planning & Dreaming**: Describing how internal models allow for mental rehearsal of actions.

---

## ⚙️ Key Technical Concepts
- **Internal Simulation**: The brain/AI creating a simplified version of the world to predict outcomes.
- **Predictive Processing**: The cycle of predicting the state, observing the result, and updating the model.
- **Mental Rehearsal**: Using the model to "dream" potential futures to optimize behavior without risk.

---

## 🤖 Robotics Relevance
- **Theoretical Foundation**: Providing the "Why" behind the need for world models in robotics.
- **Cognitive Architecture**: Inspiring the design of agents that can plan and reason before acting.
- **Generalization**: Understanding how a general world model can be applied to diverse robotic tasks.
